31st International Rexx Language Symposium to be held 25 to 28 September, 2020, Online

After consulting the board and some of the membership, it was decided that the 31st Symposium will be an online only symposium. This will be the first time this symposium will not be held on location. For this year IBM Austin has graciously provided a room, but it was not to be due to the COVID-19 crisis.
